Egwusi and Semo

the most popular Nigerian food. Egusi soup with semo. So Yummy

INGREDIENTS


  • Golden Penny Semovita

  • 2 Cups Melon

  • 1 Cup Palm Oil or a little more or less depending on preference

  • 4 Tbsp Crayfish

  • 3 Tbsp locust beans

  • Chopped spinach Ugu

  • 1 Red Bell Pepper Tatashe

  • 1 Habanero Pepper Ata Rodo

  • 1 Fresno Pepper Shombo

  • 2 Onions

  • Stock Cube Optional

  • 1 lb Beef Tripe Assorted meat, Dry Fish and Cow Leg

  • Salt To Taste

Cooking Instructions


Step 1

Make your semo swallow and keep aside. Blend the cray fish and also keep aside. Cut the leaves and also keep aside.

Step 2

Cook the meat and stock fish together until tender.wash the smoked fish and remove thorns and add into the pot.

Step 3

Put oil in a pot on fire and allow the oil to be hot,add palm oil and allow to be heated. Add your blended vegetable and then add egusi and fry, then add cray fish

Step 4

Add cooked meat,stock fish,pbomo and smoked fish and then stir. Add their stock and allow to boil.

Step 5

Add seasonings and leaves then close the pot and allow to cook till it is done


The meal is best serve with pounded yam, amala, eba, tuwo or any other swallow of choic
